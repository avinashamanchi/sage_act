Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the app for?
A: A simple planning app where you can outline your day and get help if needed
Q: How do I use the app?
A: Simply create yourself a profile and get started using all the features by clicking on buttons in the home screen
Q: Why make this?
A: We feel that Senior Citizens, like you all using this app, may need help with their day to day and this app is the perfect way of planning things out for yourself.
